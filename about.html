About

I am Christian Garcia, founder and photographer at CPhotography! My goal is to provide excellent service to all of my clients through photography. My images capture stories and memories that last for a life time, and that's all I ever want to aim for. A happy customer is a satisfied customer. I want only the absolute best for anyone and everyone in need of a photographer.

To me, photography is necessary to capture unforgettable moments in our lives. We live through so many experiences that sometimes, it's necessary to keep a souvenir/memento for these important occasions. Every now and then, people want to remember the past and of all the great times one has experienced, and that is my purpose. I want people to look at my pictures and enjoy the memories.

Growing up, I realized I didn't have many childhood and family photos to look back on. I didn't know who many of my relatives were/what they looked like. I knew I wanted my kids to be able to say differently. That's when I had the idea to purchase a camera and start documenting parts of my life and the loved ones involved in it; it gradually grew into wanting to do the same for others!
